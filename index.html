2. M.2 SSD Storage

With M.2 SSD Storage, it is pretty complex. It has 6 parts. The first one is the turbo fan, which is a fan to keep the M.2 SSD cool because the M.2 SSD can get hot very fast and easily. They are at lightning speeds, and we call that a heat sink. The next part is the aluminum fin, which holds the turbo fan and has an opening to let the M.2 SSD get cool with the holder so it does not fall out. After that, we have the M.2 SSD. With M.2, there is one very long part like a highlighter, and that is storage. Lastly, we have the bottom of the M.2 SSD, which has the job of keeping everything together with screws. You screw them in, and then you're good to go.
